# CLAUDE.md — Georgia Coworking Spaces

This project records information about visited coworking spaces in Georgia (the country), split across two files with a clear separation of concerns.

**`data.yaml`:** Factual, publicly-available data (pricing, hours, website, etc.) that could eventually be collected automatically from sources like the place's website or Google Maps. Fields like Google Places ID, address, coordinates, and ratings are planned but not yet added.

**`notes.md`:** Personal observations from actual visits — both objective (access method, staff presence, amenities) and subjective (pros, cons, overall feeling). This is the irreplaceable part that requires a human visit and cannot be automated.

The eventual destination for this data is [Giraffe](https://github.com/weibeld/giraffe/), a planned application for collecting and managing table-like datasets.
